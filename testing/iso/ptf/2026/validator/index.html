Test Form Validator

For Internal Use Only

Validate and fix structural issues in PDF test forms for compatibility with PDF/X-4 standards and older Adobe Acrobat versions.

Overview

Drop a generated PDF to analyze its structure against PDF/X-4 rules. The validator detects missing page boxes, output intent issues, optional content problems, and other structural defects.

Fixable issues can be corrected with a single click. The fixed PDF is re-validated to confirm all corrections were applied.
